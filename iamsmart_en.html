iAM Smart Application Usage and Feedback

This survey aims to understand Hong Kong residents' usage and opinions of the iAM Smart application to improve its design and functionality. All responses are completely anonymous, and no personally identifiable information will be collected. The survey takes approximately 5 minutes to complete.

This study is part of a CUHK SEEM Department FYP Project, focusing on the user experience and potential improvements of the iAM Smart application. Responses are collected anonymously and cannot be traced back to individuals. Electronic data will be stored until December 2026 and then deleted. The research team will make every effort to protect your privacy and maintain confidentiality, minimizing any potential risks.

You are free to decide whether to participate and may withdraw at any time without pressure. However, once submitted, as the survey is anonymous and untraceable, the research team will be unable to identify or delete your responses.

The study will be analyzed in aggregate, and no individual will be identified. Results will be published in an academic report with no commercial interests involved.
