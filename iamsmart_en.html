iAM Smart App Usage and Opinions Survey

iAM Smart App Usage and Opinions Survey

This questionnaire aims to understand Hong Kong residents’ usage and opinions of the current “iAM Smart” app, in order to improve its design and functions. All answers are completely anonymous and no personally identifiable information will be collected. It takes about 5 minutes to complete.

This study is part of a Final Year Project in the CUHK SEEM Department, mainly exploring user experience and improvement suggestions for the “iAM Smart” app. Responses are anonymous and untraceable. Electronic data will be retained until December 2026 and then deleted. The research team will do its utmost to protect your privacy and confidentiality and minimise any potential risks.

You are free to decide whether to participate and may stop at any time without pressure. However, once submitted, since the questionnaire is anonymous and has no tracking code, the research team will not be able to identify or delete your submitted responses.

Future results will be published in aggregate form and provided to ITSC for reference. Research data will not reveal individual identities and will only be used for academic reporting purposes with no commercial interest involved.
